About Me

Engineering Leader | Building Scalable Systems for Intelligent Workflows

My experience spans aerospace, automotive, and heavy equipment programs, where I have led simulation-driven initiatives that improved design confidence, reduced dependence on physical testing, and accelerated engineering cycles. My work sits at the intersection of structural engineering, advanced simulation, and AI-enabled modeling, with a strong focus on validation, risk, and decision readiness.

More recently, I have focused on physics-based AI and machine learning—not as isolated technical efforts, but as scalable enterprise capabilities aligned with engineering processes, governance, and business constraints.

Beyond technical execution, I operate at the program and organizational level, shaping simulation and AI adoption strategies, translating engineering complexity for leadership, balancing innovation with risk and delivery, and supporting decisions with clear, defensible insights.
